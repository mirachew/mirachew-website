Hello! I'm Mira

I’m a mechanical engineer with experience across precision robotics, automation, and durable mechanical systems. At Johnson & Johnson Surgical Robotics, I designed custom mechanisms, actuators, and surgical instruments. At Farm-ng, I engineered manufacturable assemblies and rugged suspension systems for autonomous tractors. My background spans mechanical design, CAD, rapid prototyping, and software integration—shaped through leading robotics teams and hands-on engineering competitions. I bring a cross-disciplinary approach to building reliable systems that perform in both high-precision and high-demand environments.
